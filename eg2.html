Scrambled eggs

Eggs are my one of the favourite dishes.

    Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 2tbsp cream

    Method

  1. Melt butter in frying pan on medium flame
  2. Gently mix eggs.
  3. Once butter is melted but eggs int it.
  4. Fold using the spatula to the center for 20 seconds.
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the pan and serve in the plate.
